Pizza Dough

The Recipe

This no-rise pizza dough recipe is quick and merely involves mixing a few basic ingredients and patting the dough into the pan â€” no need to wait for the dough to rise with this approach.

Using this recipe, you can easily use this dough along with your other favorite ingredients to make you very own awesome pizza! Collect some of your favorite sauces and toppings and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Mix in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
  2. To bake the dough, preheat the oven to 375 degrees C (190 degrees C).
  3. Pat and stretch dough onto a large pizza pan. Cover with desired toppings.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is lightly browned, 20 to 25 minutes.
